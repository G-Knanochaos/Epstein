About

This website is an interactive game that challenges users to guess which individuals are mentioned more frequently in publicly released court documents related to the Jeffrey Epstein case.

The data used on this site is derived from publicly available documents. Appearance in these documents does not imply wrongdoing.

All mention-count numbers shown in the game are pulled from publicly released U.S. Department of Justice Epstein case documents available at https://www.justice.gov/epstein.
